Title: Finding Nexus Bank ATMs
Category: branch
Doc-ID: branch-002

Nexus Bank operates over 2,000 ATMs nationwide, and customers can also use partner network ATMs with reduced or no fees.

Finding a Nexus Bank ATM:
- App: Tap "Branches & ATMs" on the home screen, then select "ATMs." Your nearest ATMs are shown on a map.
- Website: Visit nexusbank.com/atm-locator and enter your location.
- SMS: Text "ATM" followed by your zip code to 90900 (e.g., "ATM 10001") and receive the 3 nearest ATM addresses.

ATM types:
- Standard ATMs: Cash withdrawal, balance inquiry, mini-statement, PIN change.
- Full-service ATMs (marked with a star in the app): All standard services + cash deposit, cheque deposit, mobile top-up, account-to-account transfer.

ATM fee structure:
- Nexus Bank ATMs: Free for all customers.
- Partner network ATMs (AllPoint, MoneyPass): Free for Gold and Platinum account holders; $2 per withdrawal for Standard accounts.
- Out-of-network ATMs: $2.50 per withdrawal + any fee charged by the ATM operator.
- International ATMs: $5 per withdrawal + foreign currency conversion (typically 1-2%).

Accessibility:
- All Nexus Bank ATMs are wheelchair accessible.
- Audio guidance and Braille keypads are available at all ATMs.
- Screen magnification available on newer full-service ATMs.

ATM availability: All ATMs operate 24/7. Planned maintenance is usually scheduled between 02:00 and 04:00 local time and is reflected in the app in advance.
