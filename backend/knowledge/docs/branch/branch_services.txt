Title: Services Available at Nexus Bank Branches
Category: branch
Doc-ID: branch-003

Nexus Bank branches offer a full range of in-person banking services that complement our digital channels.

Services available at all branches:
- Account opening (personal and business)
- Cash deposits and withdrawals (including large cash handling)
- Cheque deposits and cashier's cheques
- Foreign currency exchange (major currencies stocked; others ordered in advance)
- Card replacement (same-day for blocked/stolen cards, requires ID)
- PIN reset assistance
- Loan and credit card applications and advice
- Safe deposit box rental
- Notary services for banking documents
- Official / stamped account statements
- Wire transfer instructions for complex international transfers
- Identity verification for online account setup

Appointment-required services:
- Mortgage consultation and application
- Investment advice and portfolio review
- Business account opening and relationship manager meeting
- Power of attorney registration
- Estate/bereavement case management

Book an appointment: App > Appointments > Branch Appointment, or call 1-800-NEXUS-00.

Accessibility:
- All branches are wheelchair accessible with ramps and accessible counters.
- Hearing loops (induction loops) available at service desks — inform the front desk on arrival.
- Language assistance: Interpreter services available for over 20 languages with advance notice (48 hours).

Paperless in-branch: All documents can be signed digitally on a branch tablet. Paper copies are available on request.
