Title: Branch Opening Hours
Category: branch
Doc-ID: branch-001

Nexus Bank branches are open during the following standard hours. Hours may vary on public holidays.

Standard branch hours:
- Monday to Friday: 09:00 – 17:00
- Saturday: 09:00 – 13:00
- Sunday: Closed

Extended-hours branches (select locations):
- Monday to Friday: 08:00 – 19:00
- Saturday: 09:00 – 16:00
- Sunday: Closed (some flagship branches open 10:00 – 14:00)

Holiday hours:
- Branches are closed on all federal public holidays.
- A schedule of holiday closures is published each year at nexusbank.com/branches and in the Nexus Bank app.
- ATMs remain operational 24/7 on public holidays.

Finding branch hours:
- Use the Branch Locator at nexusbank.com/branches or in the Nexus Bank app under "Branches & ATMs."
- Search by city, zip code, or your current location.
- Each branch listing shows its specific hours, services, and accessibility features.

Appointment-only services:
- Mortgage consultations, business account opening, and investment advice require appointments even during open hours.
- Book via the app under Appointments, or call 1-800-NEXUS-00.

Self-service areas:
- Many Nexus Bank branches have a self-service zone accessible outside branch hours (ATMs, deposit machines, statement printing) — check the branch listing for availability.
