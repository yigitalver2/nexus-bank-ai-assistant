Title: Booking a Branch Appointment
Category: branch
Doc-ID: branch-005

For complex banking needs, booking an appointment in advance ensures you get dedicated time with the right specialist.

When to book an appointment:
- Mortgage or home equity consultation.
- Business account setup and relationship manager meeting.
- Investment portfolio review or financial planning session.
- Estate and bereavement case discussions.
- Complaint escalation requiring in-person resolution.
- Power of attorney or legal document registration.
- Foreign currency large orders.

How to book:

Via the app:
1. Tap "Branches & ATMs" > select a branch > "Book Appointment."
2. Choose the appointment type, preferred date, and time.
3. Receive a confirmation with the specialist's name and branch details.

Via the website:
- nexusbank.com/appointments — same process as the app.

Via phone:
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 and ask for an appointment. Representative will find available slots for your preferred branch and purpose.

What to bring:
- Valid government-issued photo ID (always required).
- Any documents relevant to your appointment (loan documents, property details, insurance policies, etc.).

Cancellation policy:
- Cancel or reschedule via app or phone at least 4 hours before the appointment.
- Repeated no-shows may require a deposit for specialist appointment slots.

Walk-ins: Walk-ins are accepted for most standard services during branch hours, subject to queue wait times. Appointments are prioritized over walk-ins.
