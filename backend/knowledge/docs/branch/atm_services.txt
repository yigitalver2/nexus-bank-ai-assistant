Title: ATM Services and Features
Category: branch
Doc-ID: branch-004

Nexus Bank ATMs offer more than just cash withdrawals. Here is a full list of available services depending on ATM type.

Standard ATM services (all Nexus ATMs):
- Cash withdrawal (up to daily limit, single withdrawal max $500)
- Balance inquiry (on-screen or printed receipt)
- Mini-statement (last 10 transactions)
- PIN change
- Mobile top-up (prepaid phone credit, select carriers)

Full-service ATM services (marked with a star in the app):
All standard services, plus:
- Cash deposit (counted and credited within 1 business hour; visible immediately as pending)
- Cheque deposit (processing begins same day if deposited before 16:00)
- Account-to-account transfer (between your own Nexus accounts)
- Standing order setup and cancellation
- Foreign currency ordering (for collection at a branch the next business day)

ATM receipts:
- Receipts are offered after every transaction.
- You can also opt for an email receipt (link your email to your card in the app under Cards > Settings > Email Receipts).
- Paper receipts should not be left at the ATM — they contain partial account information.

ATM deposit limits:
- Cash: Up to $10,000 per day.
- Cheques: Up to $5,000 per day (5 cheques maximum).

If an ATM swallows your deposit:
- Note the ATM ID (printed on the machine) and the exact time.
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 immediately.
- We will investigate and credit the correct amount within 3 business days.
