Title: How to Dispute a Transaction
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-004

If you notice an unauthorized, incorrect, or duplicate transaction on your account, you can raise a dispute.

Types of disputes:
- Unauthorized transaction: A charge you did not make and did not authorize.
- Duplicate charge: The same transaction charged more than once.
- Incorrect amount: You were charged more than the agreed price.
- Non-receipt of goods/services: You paid but the merchant never delivered.
- Merchant refund not received: The merchant confirmed a refund that hasn't appeared on your account.

How to dispute:

Via the mobile app (fastest):
1. Go to Transactions, find the transaction you want to dispute.
2. Tap the transaction, then tap "Dispute this Transaction."
3. Select the reason and provide a brief description.
4. Submit. You will receive a case reference number.

Via secure message:
- Log in, go to Help > Secure Message, and describe the transaction and reason.

Via phone:
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-00. Have the transaction date, amount, and merchant name ready.

What happens next:
- Provisional credit: For unauthorized transactions, we typically credit your account within 5 business days while the investigation is ongoing.
- Investigation: We investigate and contact the merchant on your behalf. This can take up to 45 days for international transactions.
- Resolution: If the dispute is upheld, the credit becomes permanent. If not upheld, the provisional credit is reversed and we provide a written explanation.

Time limit: Disputes must be raised within 60 days of the statement date. Raise disputes promptly.
