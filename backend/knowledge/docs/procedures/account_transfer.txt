Title: Transferring Your Account to Nexus Bank (Bank Switch)
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-010

Nexus Bank participates in the Current Account Switch Service (CASS), which makes switching your main bank account from another bank quick and simple.

What the switch includes:
- Transfer of all active direct debits and standing orders to your new Nexus account.
- Redirection of all incoming payments (salary, government payments, transfers) for 36 months.
- Closure of your old account.
- Any payments made to your old account during the 36-month redirection window will still reach you.

How to switch:

Step 1 — Open a Nexus Checking account (if you haven't already).
Step 2 — Log in to online banking and go to My Accounts > Switch to Nexus.
Step 3 — Provide the details of your old account (bank name, sort code, account number).
Step 4 — Select a switch date (minimum 7 working days in the future).
Step 5 — Your old bank is notified and the switch begins on the selected date.

Timeline: The full switch takes 7 working days from the start date.

What you need to do:
- Keep enough funds in your old account until the switch date.
- Update your new account details with any billers who pay you (e.g., employer) — the redirection service handles those who fail to update.

Switching bonus:
- New customers who switch their main account to Nexus receive a $200 welcome bonus after completing 2 direct debits within 60 days.

For partial account transfers (e.g., moving savings without closing the old account), see account management options under My Accounts.
