Title: Claiming Funds from a Deceased Customer's Account
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-009

When an account holder passes away, Nexus Bank works with the designated beneficiaries or legal representatives to release funds.

Notifying the bank:
- Contact us as soon as possible at 1-800-NEXUS-00 (option 5) or visit any branch.
- We will immediately freeze the account to protect the estate.

Required documents:
- Death certificate (original or certified copy).
- Your government-issued photo ID.
- Proof of your legal authority: Will, probate grant, letters of administration, or other court-issued document.
- If there is a named beneficiary on the account: Their ID and a completed beneficiary claim form.

Process:
1. Submit all documents to our bereavement team in person or by certified post.
2. A dedicated bereavement case manager will be assigned within 2 business days.
3. Document review and account assessment: 5-10 business days.
4. Funds released to the estate or beneficiaries after all checks are complete.

Small estates process:
- If the total estate value at Nexus Bank is below $25,000, a simplified process applies. Contact us for details.

Joint accounts:
- If the account is held jointly (e.g., with a spouse), the surviving account holder retains full access after providing a death certificate.

Bereavement team: bereavement@nexusbank.com | 1-800-NEXUS-00 (option 5) | Monday–Friday 09:00–17:00.
