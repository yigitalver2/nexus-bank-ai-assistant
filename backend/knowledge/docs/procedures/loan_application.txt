Title: Loan Application Process
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-002

Applying for a Nexus Bank loan (personal, vehicle, or mortgage) follows a structured process.

Step 1 — Choose your loan type and check eligibility:
- Use the Loan Calculator at nexusbank.com/loans to estimate monthly payments and rates.
- Check if you meet the minimum requirements (income, credit score, residency).

Step 2 — Submit the application:
- Online: Log in and go to Products > Loans > Apply.
- App: Go to Borrow > Apply for a Loan.
- Branch: Visit any Nexus branch and speak to a loan advisor.

Step 3 — Provide required documents:
- Government-issued photo ID.
- Proof of income: Last 3 months of payslips, or last 2 years of tax returns (self-employed).
- Proof of residence: Utility bill or bank statement from the last 3 months.
- For vehicle loans: Vehicle details or dealer quote.
- For mortgages: Property purchase agreement and additional documentation.

Step 4 — Credit assessment:
- A hard credit inquiry will be performed.
- Assessment typically takes 1 business day (personal/vehicle) or up to 10 days (mortgage).

Step 5 — Decision and offer:
- Approved: You receive a loan offer with the rate, term, and monthly payment. Review and sign digitally.
- Conditionally approved: Additional documents may be requested.
- Declined: A written explanation will be provided. You may reapply after 6 months.

Step 6 — Disbursement:
- Personal loan: Credited to your Nexus account within 24 hours of signing.
- Vehicle loan: Paid directly to dealer.
- Mortgage: Disbursed at closing.
