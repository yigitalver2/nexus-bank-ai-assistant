Title: How to Update Your Address and Contact Details
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-005

Keeping your contact information up to date ensures you receive statements, cards, and important notifications.

Updating your address:

Via online banking:
1. Log in and go to Profile > Personal Details > Address.
2. Enter your new address and confirm.
3. An SMS confirmation is sent to your registered mobile number.
4. Your new address is active immediately for digital communications. Cards and statements will be sent to the new address within 1-2 business days.

Via app:
1. Go to Profile (person icon) > Edit Details.
2. Tap "Update Address" and fill in the new information.
3. Confirm with biometric or PIN.

Via branch:
- Visit any branch with proof of new address (utility bill or government letter dated within 3 months) and photo ID.

Note: For mortgage customers, address changes must be confirmed in writing due to legal requirements. Please visit a branch or send a secure message.

Updating your phone number:
- Phone number changes require in-branch verification or a call to 1-800-NEXUS-00, as your phone number is used for 2FA.

Updating your email address:
1. Log in to online banking > Profile > Email.
2. Enter the new email. A verification link is sent to both old and new email addresses.
3. Click the link in the new email to complete the change.

For legal name changes (e.g., after marriage), see the "Name Change" procedure.
