Title: Credit Card Application Process
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-003

Applying for a Nexus Bank credit card is straightforward and can be done fully online.

Step 1 — Compare cards:
- Visit nexusbank.com/cards to compare the Classic, Gold, and Platinum cards.
- Use the eligibility checker to see which card you are likely to be approved for (soft credit check, does not affect your credit score).

Step 2 — Apply:
- Online: Click "Apply Now" on the card page.
- App: Go to Products > Credit Cards > Apply.
- Branch: Speak to an advisor who will process the application.

Step 3 — Provide required information:
- Personal details: Full name, date of birth, address, national ID or Social Security Number.
- Financial details: Annual income, employment status, employer name, monthly housing costs.
- Existing debts: Approximate total of any other credit cards, loans, or overdrafts.

Step 4 — Credit check and decision:
- A hard credit inquiry is performed upon submission.
- Decision is typically instant (95% of applications). Complex cases may take up to 3 business days.

Step 5 — Receive your card:
- If approved, your card is mailed within 5-7 business days.
- Activate the card via the app or by calling 1-800-NEXUS-00.
- Add to your digital wallet immediately for instant use while the physical card is in transit.

Step 6 — If declined:
- You will receive a written explanation.
- You may request a manual review within 30 days if you believe the decision was incorrect.
- Improving credit score and reapplying after 6 months is recommended.
