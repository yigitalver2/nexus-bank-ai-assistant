Title: How to Upgrade Your Account
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-007

Nexus Bank offers account upgrades to give customers access to better rates, higher limits, and premium services as their financial needs grow.

Account upgrade paths:
- Standard Checking → Gold Checking (requires average balance of $5,000+ or salary deposit $3,000+/month)
- Savings → Premium Savings (requires $10,000 minimum balance)
- Classic Credit Card → Gold Card (requires income verification and credit review)
- Gold Credit Card → Platinum Card (requires income $80,000+ and good credit history)

How to request an upgrade:

Via the app:
1. Go to My Accounts > the account you want to upgrade.
2. Tap "Upgrade Account" if available.
3. Review the new terms and confirm.
4. Some upgrades are automatic if you meet the criteria.

Via online banking:
1. Go to My Accounts > Account Settings > Upgrade Options.
2. Select the desired tier and follow the prompts.

Via phone or branch:
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 or visit any branch and speak to a relationship manager.

What changes after an upgrade:
- New fees (or fee waivers) take effect from the next billing cycle.
- New limits (e.g., transfer limits, ATM limits) apply immediately.
- For credit card upgrades, a new physical card is issued within 5-7 business days.
- Your account number and IBAN do not change.

Downgrading: If you no longer meet the criteria for a premium tier, you will be notified 30 days in advance before your account is moved to the lower tier.
