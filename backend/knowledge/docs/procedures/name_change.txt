Title: How to Update Your Name on Your Account
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-006

A legal name change (due to marriage, divorce, court order, or other legal reason) must be reflected on your Nexus Bank account.

Required documents:
- Marriage certificate (for name change due to marriage).
- Divorce decree (if reverting to a previous name).
- Court order (for any other legal name change).
- Updated government-issued photo ID in your new name (passport or national ID card).

Process:
1. Visit any Nexus Bank branch in person — name changes cannot be processed online or by phone due to legal identity requirements.
2. Bring all required documents (originals or certified copies).
3. Complete the "Name Change Request" form at the branch.
4. A branch representative will verify the documents and update your account.

Timeline:
- Account name updated: Immediately at the branch.
- New debit/credit cards issued in the new name: Mailed within 5-7 business days.
- Updated statements: Next billing cycle onwards.

Impact on linked services:
- Your IBAN and account number remain the same.
- Direct debits and standing orders are not affected.
- Inform any employers, government agencies, or billers of your name change separately.

There is no fee for a name change request. If you require an updated account confirmation letter in your new name for legal purposes, this can be printed at the branch at no charge.
