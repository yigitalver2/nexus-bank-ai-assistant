Title: How to File a Complaint
Category: procedures
Doc-ID: proc-001

Nexus Bank takes all complaints seriously. If you are not satisfied with our service, here is how to raise a formal complaint.

Step 1 — Contact the relevant team:
- First, contact the team responsible for the issue (e.g., cards, loans, transfers) via phone, chat, or secure message.
- Many issues are resolved at this stage within 1 business day.

Step 2 — Escalate to the Complaints Team:
- If your issue is not resolved satisfactorily, submit a formal complaint:
  - Email: complaints@nexusbank.com
  - Post: Nexus Bank Complaints, 200 Financial Plaza, Suite 100, New York, NY 10004
  - In person: At any branch, ask for a Complaint Form.
- Include: your full name, account number, description of the issue, what happened, and your desired resolution.

Step 3 — Our response:
- Acknowledgment within 2 business days.
- Full response within 15 business days (we will update you if more time is needed).
- Final response always provided within 35 business days.

Step 4 — External escalation:
- If you are still not satisfied after our final response, you may escalate to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) at consumerfinance.gov or call 1-855-411-2372.

Your rights:
- You have the right to a written explanation of any decision that affects your account.
- Complaints do not affect your banking relationship or credit file.
