Title: ATM Withdrawal Limits and ATM Usage
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-011

Nexus Bank ATMs are available 24/7 and allow a range of transactions beyond cash withdrawal.

Daily ATM withdrawal limits:
- Standard accounts: $1,000/day
- Gold accounts: $2,000/day
- Platinum accounts: $5,000/day

Single transaction limit: $500 per withdrawal (you can make multiple withdrawals up to your daily limit).

Nexus Bank ATM services:
- Cash withdrawal
- Balance inquiry
- Mini-statement (last 10 transactions)
- PIN change
- Mobile top-up
- Cheque deposit (at enabled ATMs)

Using non-Nexus ATMs:
- Within the country: $2 fee per withdrawal (waived for Premium and above).
- International ATMs: $5 fee per withdrawal + applicable foreign currency conversion fee.

If an ATM retains your card:
- The machine will return retained cards at end of day.
- If not returned, call 1-800-NEXUS-00 immediately to block your card and request a replacement.
- Do not attempt to force the card out of the machine.

If the ATM dispenses incorrect cash:
- Note the time, location, and amount displayed on screen.
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 and we will investigate. Your account will be corrected within 3 business days.
