Title: Nexus Bank Mobile App — Common Issues and Help
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-006

The Nexus Bank mobile app is available for iOS 14+ and Android 8+.

Common issues and solutions:

App won't load / blank screen:
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
- Force-close the app and reopen.
- Update the app to the latest version from the App Store / Google Play.
- If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app (your data is saved on the server).

Biometric login not working:
- Go to Settings > Security > Biometric Login and disable, then re-enable.
- Ensure your device biometrics are up-to-date in phone settings.

Push notifications not received:
- Check that notifications are enabled for the Nexus Bank app in your phone settings.
- Log out and log back in to re-register the push token.

Transfer not appearing:
- Allow up to 5 minutes for transactions to reflect.
- Pull down to refresh the transaction list.
- Scheduled transfers appear with a "Pending" label until processed.

To report a bug or technical issue in the app, go to Settings > Help > Report an Issue. This sends a diagnostic log to our technical team.

For urgent issues, call 1-800-NEXUS-00.
