Title: Foreign Currency Exchange and International Transfers
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-010

Nexus Bank supports over 30 foreign currencies for exchange and international wire transfers.

Currency exchange:
- Available at all branches and via online banking (My Accounts > Currency Exchange).
- Exchange rates are updated in real-time during business hours (09:00–17:00 weekdays).
- A spread of 0.5%–2% applies depending on the currency and amount.
- Premium and Platinum account holders receive preferential rates.

International wire transfers:
- Sending: Go to Payments > International Transfer, enter the recipient's IBAN/SWIFT/BIC, amount, and currency.
- Fee: $15 flat for amounts up to $5,000; $25 for amounts $5,001–$50,000; negotiated for higher amounts.
- Processing time: 1–3 business days (SEPA) or 3–5 business days (SWIFT).

SEPA transfers (within the EU/EEA):
- Available in EUR only.
- Fee: $5 flat.
- Processing time: next business day.

Incoming international transfers:
- Your international account details (IBAN + SWIFT: NEXUSBANKXX) are available in My Accounts > Account Details.
- Incoming transfers are typically credited within 1-2 business days.

Note: Some countries have currency controls or sanctions restrictions. Transfers to restricted countries will be declined. Contact support for clarification.
