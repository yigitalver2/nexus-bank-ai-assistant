Title: How to Open a New Account
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-001

To open a new account at Nexus Bank, follow these steps:

1. Visit any Nexus Bank branch with a valid government-issued ID (passport or national ID card).
2. Alternatively, use our mobile app or website to start an online application.
3. Provide your full name, date of birth, residential address, and tax identification number.
4. Choose your account type: Checking, Savings, or Premium Savings.
5. Make an initial deposit of at least $25 for Checking or $100 for Savings accounts.

Account opening is completed within 1 business day for online applications, or immediately in-branch. You will receive your debit card within 5-7 business days by mail.

For assistance, contact support at support@nexusbank.com or call 1-800-NEXUS-00.
