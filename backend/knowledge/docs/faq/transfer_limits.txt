Title: Transfer Limits and Daily Transaction Caps
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-004

Nexus Bank applies daily transfer limits to protect customers from fraud. Limits vary by account tier:

Standard Checking / Savings:
- Online transfers to own accounts: $10,000/day
- Online transfers to third parties: $5,000/day
- ATM cash withdrawal: $1,000/day
- POS (point-of-sale) purchases: $3,000/day

Premium Savings / Gold accounts:
- Online transfers to own accounts: $25,000/day
- Online transfers to third parties: $15,000/day
- ATM cash withdrawal: $2,000/day
- POS purchases: $10,000/day

Platinum accounts:
- Online transfers to own accounts: Unlimited
- Online transfers to third parties: $50,000/day (higher limits available on request)
- ATM cash withdrawal: $5,000/day
- POS purchases: Unlimited

To request a temporary limit increase (e.g., for a large purchase or property payment), contact support at least 24 hours in advance. We may request supporting documentation for amounts above $50,000.

International wire transfers have separate limits — please see the "Foreign Currency Exchange" FAQ.
