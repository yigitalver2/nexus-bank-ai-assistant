Title: Cheque Services — Requesting and Depositing Cheques
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-020

Nexus Bank supports cheque issuance for eligible account holders and cheque deposits via branch or ATM.

Requesting a cheque book:
- Cheque books are available for Standard Checking, Business Checking, and Premium accounts.
- Request via online banking: My Accounts > Cheques > Order Cheque Book.
- Delivery: 5-7 business days. Collect from branch if you need it sooner.
- Cost: First cheque book (25 leaves) is free. Subsequent books: $5 each.

Writing a cheque:
- Fill in the payee name, date, and amount (in words and figures).
- Sign as per your account signature on file.
- Cross the cheque with two parallel lines to prevent cash encashment ("Account Payee Only").

Depositing a cheque:
- In branch: Hand the cheque to a teller with your deposit slip.
- At ATM: Use enabled ATMs with cheque deposit slots.
- Clearing time: Domestic cheques clear within 2 business days. Foreign cheques: up to 21 days.

Stopping a cheque payment:
- If a cheque has not yet been presented, you can stop it.
- Go to My Accounts > Cheques > Stop Cheque Payment, or call 1-800-NEXUS-00.
- Fee: $10 per stopped cheque.

Bounced cheques:
- If your account has insufficient funds when a cheque is presented, it will bounce.
- Fee: $25 for a returned cheque.
- Repeated bounced cheques may result in cheque book privileges being revoked.
