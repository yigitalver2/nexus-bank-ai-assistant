Title: How to Block or Freeze Your Card
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-003

If your card is lost, stolen, or you suspect unauthorized use, you can block it immediately through the following methods:

Via the Nexus Bank Mobile App (fastest):
1. Open the app and go to "Cards" in the bottom navigation.
2. Select the card you want to block.
3. Tap "Block Card" and confirm.
The card is blocked instantly. You can unblock it the same way if it was a mistake.

Via the website:
1. Log in and navigate to My Cards > Manage Card.
2. Select "Temporarily Block" for lost cards or "Permanently Block" if stolen.

Via phone:
Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 (available 24/7). After identity verification, a representative will block your card immediately.

Important: Temporarily blocking a card stops all new transactions but does not affect pending transactions already authorized. Permanently blocking a card cannot be undone — a new card will be issued within 5-7 business days.

If you believe fraud has occurred, also report it using the "Dispute a Transaction" option in the app.
