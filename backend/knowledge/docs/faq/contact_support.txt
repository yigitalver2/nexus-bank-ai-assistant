Title: How to Contact Nexus Bank Support
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-019

Nexus Bank offers multiple support channels to assist customers.

Phone support:
- General inquiries: 1-800-NEXUS-00 (1-800-639-8700) — 24/7
- Fraud and card emergencies: 1-800-NEXUS-FR — 24/7
- Business banking: 1-800-NEXUS-BZ — Mon–Fri 08:00–20:00
- International callers: +1-555-NEXUS-00

Email support:
- General: support@nexusbank.com (response within 24 hours)
- Complaints: complaints@nexusbank.com (response within 3 business days, as required by regulation)

Live chat:
- Available on the website and mobile app.
- Hours: Mon–Sat 08:00–22:00, Sun 10:00–18:00.
- AI assistant available 24/7 for common queries.

Secure message (online banking):
- Log in and go to Help > Secure Message.
- Best for account-specific queries that require verification.
- Response within 1 business day.

Branch visits:
- Find your nearest branch using the Branch Locator in the app or at nexusbank.com/branches.
- Standard hours: Mon–Fri 09:00–17:00, Sat 09:00–13:00.

Social media:
- @NexusBank on Twitter/X and LinkedIn — public queries only, no account details.
- Do not share personal or financial information over social media.
