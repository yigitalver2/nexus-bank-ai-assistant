Title: Scheduled and Future-Dated Payments
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-016

Nexus Bank allows you to schedule one-time or recurring payments in advance.

Scheduling a future-dated payment:
1. Go to Payments > New Transfer.
2. Fill in the recipient, amount, and reference.
3. Under "Payment Date," toggle to "Schedule for Later."
4. Select a future date (up to 12 months in advance).
5. Confirm with 2FA.

The payment will be shown as "Scheduled" in your transaction list and will be processed on the selected date, provided sufficient funds are available.

If funds are insufficient on the scheduled date:
- The payment will fail and you will be notified by push notification and email.
- A failed payment fee of $3 may apply.

Editing or cancelling a scheduled payment:
- Go to Payments > Scheduled Payments.
- Select the payment and choose "Edit" or "Cancel."
- Changes must be made at least 1 business day before the payment date.

Recurring payments (standing orders):
- See "Setting Up Direct Debit and Standing Orders" for more details.

Payment processing times:
- Domestic transfers: Same day if submitted before 17:00 on a business day; next day otherwise.
- International transfers: 1-5 business days depending on destination.
