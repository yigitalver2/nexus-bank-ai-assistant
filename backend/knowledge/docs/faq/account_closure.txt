Title: How to Close a Bank Account
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-007

To close a Nexus Bank account, please follow the steps below:

Requirements before closing:
- Account balance must be zero or you must withdraw all remaining funds.
- All pending transactions must be settled.
- Any linked direct debits or standing orders must be cancelled or transferred.
- Active loans, credit cards, or overdrafts linked to the account must be settled first.

How to close:
Option 1 — In branch: Visit any Nexus Bank branch with a valid photo ID. A relationship manager will process the closure after confirming the above conditions are met.

Option 2 — Via secure message: Log in to online banking, go to Help > Secure Message, and submit a "Request Account Closure" message. We will respond within 2 business days and guide you through the process.

Note: You cannot close an account if it is the primary account linked to an active loan or credit card. You must first transfer those products to another account.

Account closure is irreversible. You will receive a final statement by email within 5 business days. If you have any questions about the process, call 1-800-NEXUS-00.
