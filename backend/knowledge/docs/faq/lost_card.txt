Title: What to Do If You Lose Your Card
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-012

If your Nexus Bank card is lost or stolen, act quickly to prevent unauthorized use.

Immediate steps:
1. Block the card via the mobile app (Cards > Block Card) or call 1-800-NEXUS-00 immediately.
2. Review recent transactions for unauthorized charges.
3. If you see fraudulent transactions, report them using the "Dispute a Transaction" flow or inform the call center representative.

Requesting a replacement card:
- Replacement cards are issued automatically when you permanently block a lost/stolen card.
- Delivery: 5-7 business days to your registered address.
- Express delivery (1-2 business days) is available for a $15 fee.
- You can also collect the card from any branch the next business day (bring your ID).

If you find the card after blocking it:
- Temporarily blocked cards can be unblocked via the app.
- Permanently blocked cards cannot be unblocked — use the replacement card when it arrives.

Costs:
- First replacement per year: Free.
- Additional replacements: $10 per card.

Always sign the back of your new card immediately upon receipt and register it in the mobile app before use.
