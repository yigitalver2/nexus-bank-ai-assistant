Title: Managing Saved Beneficiaries for Transfers
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-015

Saving beneficiaries (recipients) makes repeat transfers faster and reduces the chance of errors.

Adding a beneficiary:
1. Log in to online banking or the mobile app.
2. Go to Payments > Beneficiaries > Add New.
3. Enter the recipient's full name, IBAN, and an optional nickname.
4. Confirm with your 2FA code.
5. New beneficiaries are placed on a 24-hour hold before the first transfer can be made (fraud prevention).

Editing a beneficiary:
- You can update the nickname at any time.
- To change the IBAN, you must delete and re-add the beneficiary. The 24-hour hold applies again.

Deleting a beneficiary:
- Go to Payments > Beneficiaries, select the contact, and tap "Delete."
- Deleting a beneficiary does not cancel any scheduled or standing order payments to them — cancel those separately.

Sending to a new (unsaved) recipient:
- You can always transfer to a new IBAN without saving them as a beneficiary.
- For amounts above $2,000 to a new recipient, an additional SMS verification is required.

Beneficiary limit: You can save up to 200 beneficiaries per account.

For bulk beneficiary import (business accounts), contact your relationship manager.
