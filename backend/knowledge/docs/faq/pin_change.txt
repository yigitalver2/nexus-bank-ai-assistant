Title: How to Change Your Card PIN
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-013

You can change the PIN for your Nexus Bank debit or credit card through several methods.

Via a Nexus Bank ATM (recommended):
1. Insert your card and enter your current PIN.
2. Select "PIN Services" from the main menu.
3. Choose "Change PIN."
4. Enter your new 4-digit PIN twice to confirm.
5. Your new PIN is active immediately.

Via the mobile app:
1. Go to Cards > Manage Card.
2. Tap "Change PIN."
3. Enter your current PIN, then your new PIN twice.
4. Confirm with biometric authentication.

Via the web portal:
1. Log in and go to My Cards > Manage Card.
2. Select "Change PIN" and follow the on-screen steps.
3. You will receive an SMS confirmation.

PIN requirements:
- Must be exactly 4 digits.
- Cannot be a sequence (1234) or repeated digits (1111).
- Cannot be your date of birth or a previously used PIN.

If you have forgotten your PIN:
- You cannot retrieve a forgotten PIN — it is never stored by the bank.
- You can set a new PIN via the mobile app or ATM after identity verification.
- For security, after 3 incorrect PIN attempts, the card will be blocked temporarily.
