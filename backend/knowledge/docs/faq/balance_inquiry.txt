Title: How to Check Your Account Balance
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-014

You can check your Nexus Bank account balance through multiple channels:

Mobile app (real-time):
- Log in and your balance is displayed on the home dashboard.
- Swipe between accounts if you have multiple.
- Balance updates are real-time for internal transactions and near-real-time for external ones.

Online banking (real-time):
- Log in at bank.nexusbank.com and view balances under My Accounts.

ATM balance inquiry:
- Insert your card, enter your PIN, and select "Balance Inquiry."
- Balance is displayed on-screen and can optionally be printed.
- No fee at Nexus Bank ATMs.

Phone banking:
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 and select the automated balance line (option 1).
- Verify with your account number and PIN.

SMS balance (optional service):
- Text "BAL" to 90900 from your registered mobile number.
- You will receive an SMS with your current balance within seconds.
- This service is free and can be enabled/disabled in app settings.

Available balance vs. total balance:
- Total balance: All funds in the account.
- Available balance: Total minus any pending transactions or holds. This is the amount you can spend right now.

If there is a discrepancy between your expected and actual balance, check for pending transactions or contact support.
