Title: How to Reset Your Online Banking Password
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-002

If you have forgotten your online banking password, you can reset it in two ways:

Option 1 — Via the website or mobile app:
1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password."
2. Enter the email address linked to your account.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link (valid for 15 minutes).
4. Follow the link and set a new password. Password must be at least 8 characters and include one number and one special character.

Option 2 — Via phone support:
1. Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 and verify your identity using your national ID and date of birth.
2. A temporary password will be sent to your registered mobile number via SMS.
3. Log in with the temporary password and change it immediately.

If you suspect your account has been compromised, call us immediately so we can freeze the account while you regain access.
