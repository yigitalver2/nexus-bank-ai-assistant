Title: Requesting Account Statements
Category: faq
Doc-ID: faq-009

Nexus Bank provides account statements in digital and paper formats.

Digital statements (free):
- Log in to online banking and go to My Accounts > Statements.
- Select the account and the date range (monthly statements going back up to 7 years).
- Download as PDF.
- Statements are also automatically emailed to your registered address each month.

Paper statements:
- Paper statements are available on request for a fee of $2 per statement.
- To request, call 1-800-NEXUS-00 or visit a branch.
- Delivery takes 5-7 business days.

Official / stamped statements for legal purposes:
- Required for visa applications, mortgage applications, or court purposes.
- Must be requested in-branch with valid photo ID.
- Takes 2-3 business days to prepare.
- Fee: $10 per document.

Transaction-level export:
- You can export your transaction history as a CSV or Excel file from My Accounts > Transactions > Export.
- Useful for personal accounting or tax purposes.

If you notice an error in a statement, report it immediately via secure message or call support.
