Title: Nexus Digital Wallet
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-011

The Nexus Digital Wallet allows you to make instant payments from your phone without carrying a physical card.

Supported platforms:
- Apple Pay (iPhone, Apple Watch, iPad, Mac).
- Google Pay (Android phones and watches).
- Nexus Pay (in-app payments via QR code, unique to Nexus customers).

Setting up:
1. Open the Nexus Bank app.
2. Go to Cards > Add to Digital Wallet.
3. Select your card and follow the device-specific setup steps.
4. Verify via SMS or biometric.

How to pay in-store:
- Hold your phone near the contactless terminal.
- Authenticate with Face ID, fingerprint, or PIN.
- Payment is processed instantly.

Nexus Pay (QR-based):
- Open the app, tap "Pay" on the home screen.
- Scan the merchant's QR code or show your QR to the merchant.
- Works at all participating Nexus Bank merchants and partner apps.
- Can also be used for person-to-person (P2P) payments.

Security:
- Your actual card number is never shared with merchants. A unique device token is used.
- Each transaction requires biometric or PIN confirmation.
- Lost phone: Block your digital wallet via the app or by calling support, without affecting the physical card.

Limits: Same daily limits as your linked card. No additional fees for digital wallet transactions.
