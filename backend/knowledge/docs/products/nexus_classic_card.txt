Title: Nexus Classic Credit Card
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-005

The Nexus Classic Credit Card is our entry-level credit card, designed for customers building or rebuilding their credit.

Key features:
- Credit limit: $500 – $5,000 (based on credit assessment).
- Annual fee: $0 (no annual fee).
- Purchase APR: 22.99% variable.
- Cash advance APR: 29.99%.
- Minimum repayment: 2% of outstanding balance or $25, whichever is greater.
- Contactless payment enabled.
- Free supplementary card for a family member.
- Online and mobile card management.

Rewards:
- 1% cashback on all purchases.
- Cashback credited to your account monthly.
- No cap on cashback earnings.

Billing:
- Monthly billing cycle.
- Statement sent to your email and available in online banking.
- Payment due: 25 days after statement date (interest-free period).
- If full balance is paid by due date, no interest is charged.

Eligibility:
- Minimum age: 18.
- Minimum income: $15,000/year.
- Credit check required.

Best for: First-time credit card holders or customers who want a simple, no-fee card with basic cashback.

To apply: Go to nexusbank.com/cards or visit any branch.
