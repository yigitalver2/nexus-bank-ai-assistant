Title: Nexus Savings Account
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-002

The Nexus Savings Account is designed to help customers grow their money with a competitive interest rate while maintaining easy access to funds.

Key features:
- Annual interest rate: 3.75% APY (updated quarterly based on market conditions).
- Interest calculated daily, credited monthly.
- No monthly maintenance fee if balance is $500 or above. $5/month if below.
- Up to 6 free withdrawals per month (regulatory limit for savings accounts); $3 fee per additional withdrawal.
- Linked to your Nexus Checking account for easy transfers.
- Eligible for automatic savings rules (e.g., round-up transfers from checking).

Minimum opening deposit: $100.

Eligibility:
- Must be 18 years or older (joint accounts available).
- Valid government-issued photo ID required.

Interest rate tiers:
- $0 – $4,999: 3.25% APY
- $5,000 – $24,999: 3.75% APY
- $25,000+: 4.10% APY

Best for: Customers building an emergency fund or saving toward a goal (vacation, home deposit, education).

Note: Savings accounts are not intended for daily transactions. For high-frequency use, pair with a Checking account.

To open: Available online, via app, or in any branch. Existing customers can open a savings account instantly from the app.
