Title: Nexus Time Deposit (Fixed-Term Deposit)
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-004

A Time Deposit (also called a fixed-term or certificate of deposit) locks your money for a fixed period in exchange for a guaranteed, higher interest rate.

Available terms:
- 1 month: 4.00% APY
- 3 months: 4.50% APY
- 6 months: 5.00% APY
- 12 months: 5.50% APY
- 24 months: 5.75% APY
- 36 months: 6.00% APY

Minimum deposit: $1,000.
Maximum deposit: Unlimited (large deposits may qualify for negotiated rates).

Key features:
- Guaranteed return — rate is locked for the full term regardless of market changes.
- Interest can be credited monthly, quarterly, or at maturity (your choice).
- Auto-renewal available: At maturity, the deposit automatically renews at the prevailing rate for the same term unless you instruct otherwise.
- FDIC-insured up to $250,000.

Early withdrawal:
- Early withdrawal is possible but incurs a penalty.
- Penalty: 50% of the interest that would have been earned for the remaining term.
- Principal is never at risk — you will always receive your full deposit back.

To open: Log in to online banking > Products > Time Deposit, or visit any branch. Funds are transferred from your linked Nexus account.

Best for: Customers with a lump sum they do not need immediate access to, seeking better returns than a savings account.
