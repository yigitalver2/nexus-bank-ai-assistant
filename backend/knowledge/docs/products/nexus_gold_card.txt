Title: Nexus Gold Credit Card
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-006

The Nexus Gold Credit Card is our mid-tier card with enhanced rewards and travel benefits.

Key features:
- Credit limit: $5,000 – $20,000 (based on credit assessment).
- Annual fee: $99/year (waived in the first year).
- Purchase APR: 19.99% variable.
- Cash advance APR: 26.99%.
- Contactless payment and virtual card for online purchases.
- 3D Secure authentication for online transactions.

Rewards:
- 2% cashback on dining and travel.
- 1.5% cashback on all other purchases.
- Sign-up bonus: $200 cashback after spending $1,000 in the first 3 months.
- Cashback credited monthly.

Travel benefits:
- No foreign transaction fees.
- Travel insurance: Up to $500,000 coverage for trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and lost luggage (when ticket is purchased with the card).
- Access to Nexus partner airport lounges (2 free visits/year).

Other perks:
- Free supplementary card for up to 2 family members.
- Extended warranty: 1 additional year on purchases covered by manufacturer's warranty.
- Purchase protection: 90-day coverage against damage or theft.

Eligibility:
- Minimum age: 21.
- Minimum income: $35,000/year.
- Good credit history required.

Best for: Frequent shoppers and occasional travelers who want meaningful rewards and travel perks without a high annual fee.
