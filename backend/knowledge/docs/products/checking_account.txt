Title: Nexus Checking Account
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-001

The Nexus Checking Account is our everyday transactional account designed for regular banking needs.

Key features:
- No monthly maintenance fee if you maintain an average balance of $500 or more, or receive a monthly direct deposit of $500+. Otherwise, $8/month.
- Free debit card with contactless payment.
- Up to $1,000/day ATM withdrawal limit.
- Up to $5,000/day online transfer to third parties.
- Access to the Nexus mobile app, online banking, and phone banking.
- Up to 2 free ATM withdrawals/month at non-Nexus ATMs; $2 per withdrawal thereafter.
- Overdraft protection available (application required, subject to credit check).
- Free domestic transfers between Nexus accounts.

Minimum opening deposit: $25.

Eligibility:
- Must be 18 years or older.
- Valid government-issued photo ID required.
- Social Security Number or Tax Identification Number required.

Interest rate: Not applicable (no interest on checking balances).

Best for: Customers who need a daily-use account for salary deposits, bill payments, shopping, and ATM access.

To open: Visit any branch, use the mobile app, or go to nexusbank.com/open-account.
