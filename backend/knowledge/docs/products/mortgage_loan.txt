Title: Nexus Mortgage Loan
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-009

The Nexus Mortgage Loan helps customers purchase or refinance residential property with competitive rates and flexible terms.

Loan details:
- Amount: $50,000 – $2,000,000.
- Term: 10, 15, 20, or 30 years.
- Down payment: Minimum 10% of property value (20% to avoid PMI).
- Rate types:
  - Fixed rate: Rate locked for the full term. Current 30-year fixed: 6.75% APR.
  - Adjustable rate (ARM): Lower initial rate, adjusts after 5 or 7 years. Current 5/1 ARM: 5.99% APR.

Additional costs:
- Origination fee: 0.5% of loan amount.
- Appraisal fee: $400–$700 (paid by applicant).
- Title insurance and closing costs: Typically 2%–4% of loan amount.
- PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance): Required if down payment < 20%. Approx. 0.5%–1% of loan amount/year, removed once equity reaches 20%.

Application process:
1. Pre-qualification: Submit income and debt information online — receive a pre-approval letter within 24 hours.
2. Property appraisal: Nexus Bank arranges an independent appraisal.
3. Underwriting: 3–10 business days.
4. Closing: Sign documents at a licensed escrow/title company.

Required documents:
- Last 2 years of tax returns and W-2s.
- Last 3 months of bank statements.
- Proof of employment.
- Property purchase agreement.

Best for: Customers buying a primary residence, vacation home, or investment property.
