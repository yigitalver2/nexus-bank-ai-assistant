Title: Nexus Personal Loan
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-008

The Nexus Personal Loan provides flexible financing for personal needs such as home renovation, medical expenses, travel, or debt consolidation.

Loan details:
- Amount: $1,000 – $50,000.
- Term: 12 to 60 months.
- Interest rate: 8.99% – 24.99% APR (fixed), depending on credit score and term.
- No collateral required (unsecured loan).
- No prepayment penalty — pay off early without fees.

Typical rates by credit profile:
- Excellent (750+): 8.99% – 12.99% APR
- Good (700–749): 13.00% – 17.99% APR
- Fair (650–699): 18.00% – 22.99% APR
- Below 650: Contact branch for assessment.

Monthly repayment example:
- $10,000 over 36 months at 12.99% APR = approx. $337/month.

Application process:
1. Apply online at nexusbank.com/loans or via the app.
2. Provide proof of income (last 3 payslips or tax return).
3. Decision within 1 business day for existing customers.
4. Funds disbursed to your Nexus account within 24 hours of approval.

Repayment:
- Fixed monthly payments by direct debit from your Nexus account.
- Extra payments or full early repayment allowed at any time with no penalty.

Best for: Customers needing a lump sum for a specific purpose with predictable monthly repayments.
