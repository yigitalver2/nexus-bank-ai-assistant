Title: Nexus Bank Insurance Products
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-014

Nexus Bank partners with leading insurance providers to offer protection products conveniently through your banking account.

Available insurance products:

1. Life Insurance (Term Life):
- Coverage: $50,000 – $2,000,000.
- Terms: 10, 20, or 30 years.
- Monthly premium: From $12/month (based on age and coverage).
- Application: Online in 10 minutes; medical exam may be required for coverage above $500,000.
- Provider: NexusLife Insurance Co.

2. Home Insurance:
- Covers: Dwelling, personal property, liability, additional living expenses.
- Premium: Starting at $45/month.
- Discount: 10% if home is linked to a Nexus mortgage.
- Provider: NexusShield Insurance.

3. Auto Insurance:
- Coverage: Comprehensive, collision, liability, uninsured motorist.
- Premium: Based on vehicle and driver profile. Get a quote in the app.
- Discount: 5% for Nexus checking account holders.
- Provider: NexusShield Insurance.

4. Payment Protection Insurance (PPI):
- Covers your loan or credit card repayments if you are unable to work due to illness, injury, or involuntary unemployment.
- Cost: 0.5% of outstanding loan balance per month.
- Claim waiting period: 30 days.

To get a quote or apply: Go to the app under Products > Insurance, or contact support. Insurance products are underwritten by third-party providers and are not bank deposits.
