Title: Nexus Investment Account
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-013

The Nexus Investment Account gives customers access to stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, and bonds through a seamlessly integrated brokerage account.

Account types:
- Individual brokerage: Taxable investment account, no contribution limits.
- Traditional IRA: Tax-deferred retirement savings; contribution limit $7,000/year ($8,000 if age 50+).
- Roth IRA: Tax-free growth; same contribution limits as Traditional IRA. Income limits apply.

Key features:
- $0 commission on US stock and ETF trades.
- Access to 5,000+ mutual funds (some with transaction fees).
- Bond and Treasury bill investments available.
- Fractional shares available for stocks priced above $50.
- Automatic dividend reinvestment (DRIP) option.
- Robo-advisor (Nexus Invest) available for hands-off portfolio management (0.25% annual advisory fee).
- Integrated tax reporting: 1099 forms generated automatically.

Minimum to open: $0 (brokerage), $500 (IRA), $1,000 (Robo-advisor).

Funding: Transfer from any Nexus bank account instantly.

Risk disclosure: Investments are not FDIC-insured and may lose value. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

To open: Log in to the app, go to Invest > Open Account, and select the account type. Opening takes approximately 5 minutes.
