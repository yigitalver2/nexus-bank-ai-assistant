Title: Nexus Business Checking Account
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-012

The Nexus Business Checking Account is designed for sole proprietors, small businesses, and corporations.

Tiers:

Nexus Business Starter:
- Monthly fee: $15 (waived with $3,000 average monthly balance or $5,000 in deposits).
- 200 free transactions/month; $0.50 per transaction thereafter.
- Cash deposit: Up to $5,000/month free; $0.25 per $100 thereafter.
- 1 free business debit card.

Nexus Business Plus:
- Monthly fee: $30 (waived with $10,000 average balance).
- 500 free transactions/month.
- Cash deposit: Up to $20,000/month free.
- Up to 5 business debit cards.
- Dedicated business banking team.
- API access for accounting integrations (QuickBooks, Xero).

Nexus Business Premium:
- Monthly fee: $75 (waived with $50,000 average balance).
- Unlimited transactions.
- Unlimited cash deposits.
- Relationship manager assigned.
- Payroll services integration.
- Priority customer support.

Required documents to open:
- Business registration certificate.
- Tax Employer Identification Number (EIN).
- Beneficial ownership information (for legal entities).
- Government-issued ID for all authorized signatories.

To open: Contact the business banking team at business@nexusbank.com or call 1-800-NEXUS-BZ.
