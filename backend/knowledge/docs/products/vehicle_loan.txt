Title: Nexus Vehicle Loan
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-010

The Nexus Vehicle Loan helps customers finance new or used vehicles — cars, motorcycles, and light commercial vehicles.

Loan details:
- Amount: $5,000 – $150,000.
- Term: 24 to 84 months.
- Interest rate: 5.99% – 14.99% APR (fixed), depending on vehicle age, loan term, and credit profile.
- No prepayment penalty.
- Vehicle serves as collateral (secured loan).

Rate guide:
- New vehicles (model year current or prior): 5.99% – 9.99% APR.
- Used vehicles (up to 5 years old): 7.99% – 12.99% APR.
- Used vehicles (6–10 years old): 10.99% – 14.99% APR.
- Vehicles older than 10 years: Not eligible.

Loan-to-value ratio:
- New vehicles: Up to 110% of dealer invoice price.
- Used vehicles: Up to 100% of market value (based on approved valuation guide).

Application process:
1. Apply online, via app, or in branch.
2. Provide dealer quote or vehicle details (for used vehicles).
3. Proof of income required.
4. Decision within 4 business hours for existing customers.
5. Funds disbursed directly to dealer or seller.

Insurance requirement:
- Comprehensive auto insurance required for the duration of the loan.
- Nexus Bank must be listed as loss payee on the policy.

Monthly repayment example:
- $25,000 over 60 months at 7.99% APR = approx. $507/month.
