Title: Nexus Premium Savings Account
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-003

The Nexus Premium Savings Account is our high-yield savings product for customers who maintain larger balances.

Key features:
- Annual interest rate: 4.75% APY (fixed for 6 months, then variable).
- No monthly fees — ever.
- Higher daily transfer limits ($15,000/day to third parties).
- Dedicated relationship manager for balances above $50,000.
- Free international wire transfer once per quarter.
- Preferential foreign currency exchange rates.
- Priority customer support line.

Minimum opening balance: $10,000.
Minimum ongoing balance to maintain Premium status: $10,000. If balance drops below this, account reverts to standard Savings account terms.

Interest rate tiers:
- $10,000 – $49,999: 4.50% APY
- $50,000 – $249,999: 4.75% APY
- $250,000+: Contact your relationship manager for negotiated rates.

Best for: Customers with significant savings looking for better returns than a standard savings account, with the flexibility of access.

Eligibility: Open to all customers; minimum balance required. Not available as a first account (must have an existing Nexus account).

To upgrade: Contact your relationship manager or visit any branch. Existing savings customers can request an upgrade via the app under My Accounts > Upgrade Account.
