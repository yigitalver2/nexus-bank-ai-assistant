Title: Nexus Student Loan
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-015

The Nexus Student Loan helps students and families finance higher education expenses at competitive rates.

Loan types:
- Undergraduate Loan: For students enrolled in accredited 4-year institutions.
- Graduate Loan: For students in master's, doctoral, or professional programs.
- Parent Loan: For parents of dependent students.

Loan details:
- Amount: $2,000/year up to $100,000 total (undergraduate); up to $150,000 (graduate).
- Terms: 5, 10, or 15 years (repayment begins 6 months after graduation).
- Interest rate: 5.49% – 11.99% APR (fixed or variable options).
- Origination fee: $0.
- No prepayment penalty.

Repayment options:
- Deferred: No payments while enrolled. Interest capitalizes at graduation.
- Interest-only: Pay interest while enrolled; principal repayment begins after graduation.
- Full repayment: Pay principal + interest immediately (lowest total cost).

Benefits for Nexus customers:
- 0.25% rate discount for enrolling in autopay.
- 0.25% additional discount for existing Nexus checking account holders.
- 6-month grace period post-graduation before repayment begins.
- Forbearance available for up to 12 months total during hardship.

Cosigner:
- A creditworthy cosigner can improve approval odds and reduce the interest rate.
- Cosigner release available after 24 consecutive on-time payments.

To apply: Go to nexusbank.com/student-loans. Requires enrollment verification from your school.
