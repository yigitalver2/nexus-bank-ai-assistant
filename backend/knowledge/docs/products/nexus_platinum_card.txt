Title: Nexus Platinum Credit Card
Category: products
Doc-ID: prod-007

The Nexus Platinum Credit Card is our premium card offering maximum rewards, exclusive benefits, and personalized service.

Key features:
- Credit limit: $20,000 – unlimited (based on credit assessment).
- Annual fee: $399/year.
- Purchase APR: 17.99% variable.
- Cash advance APR: 24.99%.
- Dedicated concierge service available 24/7.

Rewards:
- 3% cashback on travel and dining.
- 2% cashback on groceries and entertainment.
- 1.5% cashback on all other purchases.
- Sign-up bonus: $500 cashback after spending $3,000 in the first 3 months.
- No cap on cashback earnings.
- Points can also be redeemed for flights, hotels, and gift cards via the Nexus Rewards portal.

Premium travel benefits:
- No foreign transaction fees.
- Comprehensive travel insurance (medical, cancellation, delay, lost baggage) — up to $2,000,000 coverage.
- Unlimited access to over 1,000 airport lounges worldwide (Priority Pass membership included).
- Global Entry / TSA PreCheck credit ($100 every 4 years).
- Hotel room upgrades at Nexus partner hotels.

Other perks:
- Up to 4 free supplementary cards.
- Extended warranty: 2 additional years.
- Purchase protection: 120-day coverage.
- Cell phone protection: Up to $800/claim.
- Annual travel credit: $300 statement credit on travel purchases.

Eligibility:
- Minimum age: 21.
- Minimum income: $80,000/year.
- Excellent credit history required (700+ credit score recommended).
