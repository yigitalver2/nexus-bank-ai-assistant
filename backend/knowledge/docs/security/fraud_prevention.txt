Title: Fraud Prevention — Protecting Your Account
Category: security
Doc-ID: sec-001

Nexus Bank uses multiple layers of security to protect your accounts. Here is what we do — and what you can do — to prevent fraud.

What Nexus Bank does:
- Real-time transaction monitoring: Every transaction is analyzed for unusual patterns. Suspicious transactions are flagged or temporarily blocked.
- Device fingerprinting: We recognize your trusted devices. Login from an unrecognized device triggers additional verification.
- Behavioral analytics: We monitor typing speed, navigation patterns, and session behavior to detect account takeover attempts.
- Automatic alerts: You receive push notifications and/or SMS for every transaction above $10 by default (configurable in app settings).
- Card controls: You can restrict your card to specific transaction types (e.g., disable international transactions when not traveling).

What you should do:
- Never share your PIN, password, or OTP with anyone — including someone claiming to be from Nexus Bank. We will never ask for these.
- Monitor your account regularly and report unfamiliar transactions immediately.
- Enable transaction notifications in the app.
- Use a unique, strong password for online banking.
- Log out of online banking after each session, especially on shared devices.
- Keep your contact details up to date so we can reach you quickly in case of suspicious activity.

If you suspect fraud:
- Call our 24/7 Fraud Line: 1-800-NEXUS-FR immediately.
- Block your card via the app.
- Change your online banking password.
- We may place a temporary hold on your account while we investigate, for your protection.
