Title: Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) — Setup and Management
Category: security
Doc-ID: sec-002

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your Nexus Bank account. It is mandatory for all accounts.

How 2FA works:
After entering your username and password, you must confirm your identity with a second factor:
- SMS OTP: A 6-digit code sent to your registered mobile number (valid for 5 minutes).
- Authenticator App: A time-based code from an app like Google Authenticator or Authy (recommended — more secure than SMS).
- Biometric: Face ID or fingerprint on a trusted device.

Setting up an authenticator app (recommended):
1. Log in to online banking and go to Profile > Security > 2FA Settings.
2. Select "Authenticator App" and click "Set Up."
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm.
5. Save your backup codes somewhere secure (printed or in a password manager) — these are needed if you lose your phone.

Changing your 2FA method:
- Go to Profile > Security > 2FA Settings > Change Method.
- Your current 2FA method is required to make changes.

If you lose access to your 2FA method (e.g., lost phone):
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-00 immediately.
- Identity verification via knowledge-based questions and document check will be required.
- 2FA will be reset and you can set it up again.

Note: If you receive a 2FA code you did not request, do not share it with anyone and change your password immediately. Someone may be attempting to access your account.
