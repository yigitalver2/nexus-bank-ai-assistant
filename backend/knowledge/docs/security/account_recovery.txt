Title: Account Recovery — Regaining Access to Your Account
Category: security
Doc-ID: sec-008

If you are locked out of your Nexus Bank online banking account, here is how to recover access.

Common reasons for lockout:
- Too many incorrect password attempts (account locked after 5 attempts).
- Too many incorrect 2FA codes (account locked after 3 attempts).
- Suspicious activity detected by our systems.
- Your account was locked at your own request.

Recovery options:

Option 1 — Self-service (fastest):
1. Go to bank.nexusbank.com or open the app.
2. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. You will receive a password reset link (valid 15 minutes).
5. Click the link, set a new password, and complete 2FA verification.
This works if you still have access to your registered email and 2FA device.

Option 2 — Phone recovery:
1. Call 1-800-NEXUS-00.
2. We will verify your identity using your account number, date of birth, and national ID number.
3. A temporary password will be sent to your registered mobile number.
4. Log in and immediately set a new permanent password.

Option 3 — In-branch recovery:
1. Visit any Nexus Bank branch with valid photo ID.
2. A branch representative will verify your identity and reset your access on the spot.
3. You will receive a temporary password and must change it upon next login.

After recovery:
- Review your recent account activity for any unauthorized transactions.
- Ensure your email, phone, and 2FA settings are up to date.
- Consider enabling biometric login on your mobile device for faster and more secure access.
