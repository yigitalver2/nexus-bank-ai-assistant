Title: Card Security — Keeping Your Debit and Credit Cards Safe
Category: security
Doc-ID: sec-005

Physical and digital card security is critical to protecting your finances.

Physical card safety:
- Sign the back of your card immediately upon receipt.
- Never write your PIN on your card or keep it in the same wallet.
- Cover the keypad when entering your PIN at ATMs and POS terminals.
- Inspect ATMs for skimming devices before inserting your card — if the card slot looks unusual, do not use it.
- Destroy old cards by cutting through the chip and magnetic strip before disposal.

Online card safety:
- Only shop on websites with "https://" and a padlock icon.
- Do not save your card details on websites you don't trust.
- Use virtual card numbers (available in the Nexus app under Cards > Virtual Card) for one-time online purchases — these can be set to expire after a single use.
- Enable 3D Secure (you will be prompted to verify purchases via app or SMS for online transactions above a set limit).

Card controls (available in the Nexus app):
- Disable contactless payments.
- Disable international transactions when not traveling.
- Set a lower spending limit for online purchases.
- Enable or disable ATM withdrawals.

Real-time alerts:
- Enable push notifications for every transaction so you catch unauthorized use immediately.

If your card is compromised:
- Block the card immediately via the app.
- Call 1-800-NEXUS-FR to report fraud.
- Dispute any unauthorized transactions (see "How to Dispute a Transaction").
- Request a new card with a new card number.
