Title: How Nexus Bank Protects Your Data
Category: security
Doc-ID: sec-007

Nexus Bank is committed to protecting your personal and financial data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

Data encryption:
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
- Data stored in our databases is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
- Card numbers are stored in tokenized form — the real card number is never stored in our application database.

Access control:
- Your account data is only accessible to staff who need it for their role.
- All staff access is logged and audited.
- We use multi-factor authentication internally for all staff.

Data retention:
- Transaction records are kept for 7 years as required by law.
- Account records are kept for 5 years after account closure.
- After retention periods, data is securely deleted.

Third-party sharing:
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.
- We share data only when required by law (e.g., tax authorities, law enforcement with valid court orders) or when necessary to provide your services (e.g., payment processors, credit bureaus).
- When we share data with third parties, we require them to maintain equivalent data protection standards.

Your rights:
- Right to access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- Right to rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations).
- Right to portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise your data rights, contact: privacy@nexusbank.com or visit any branch.
