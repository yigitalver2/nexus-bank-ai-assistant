Title: Privacy Policy Summary
Category: legal
Doc-ID: legal-001

This is a plain-language summary of the Nexus Bank Privacy Policy. The full policy is available at nexusbank.com/privacy.

What data we collect:
- Identity data: Name, date of birth, national ID number, photo ID.
- Contact data: Email address, phone number, mailing address.
- Financial data: Account balances, transaction history, credit history, loan details.
- Technical data: IP address, device ID, browser type, app usage data.
- Communications data: Records of calls, emails, and chat sessions with our support team.

Why we collect it:
- To provide and manage your banking products and services.
- To comply with legal and regulatory obligations (anti-money laundering, tax reporting, etc.).
- To protect against fraud and unauthorized access.
- To improve our products and personalize your experience (with your consent where required).
- To communicate important account updates and marketing (you can opt out of marketing at any time).

Who we share it with:
- Regulators and law enforcement (when legally required).
- Credit bureaus (for credit checks and reporting).
- Payment processors and correspondent banks (to execute your transactions).
- Technology providers under strict data processing agreements.
- We never sell your data to third parties for marketing.

Your rights:
Contact privacy@nexusbank.com to exercise any of your privacy rights (access, rectification, deletion, portability, objection). We respond within 30 days.

Last updated: January 2025. Full policy: nexusbank.com/privacy.
