Title: Cookie Policy
Category: legal
Doc-ID: legal-004

This policy explains how Nexus Bank uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on its website and mobile app.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

Types of cookies we use:

1. Strictly necessary cookies:
Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled.
Examples: Session tokens (keeping you logged in), CSRF protection tokens, load-balancing cookies.

2. Functional cookies:
Remember your preferences to improve your experience.
Examples: Language preference, remembered username (if you opt in), last-visited branch.

3. Analytics cookies:
Help us understand how customers use our website so we can improve it.
Examples: Google Analytics (anonymized), internal heatmap tools. No personally identifiable information is collected.

4. Marketing cookies (opt-in only):
Used to show you relevant Nexus Bank products on third-party websites.
Examples: Retargeting pixels. We do not share this data with unaffiliated third parties.

Managing cookies:
- You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via the cookie settings icon at the bottom of our website.
- Disabling functional or analytics cookies will not prevent you from using online banking but may affect some features.
- Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via browser settings.

Third-party cookies:
Some features (e.g., embedded maps for branch locator, social media share buttons) may set third-party cookies. We do not control these. Refer to the respective third-party's privacy policy.

Full cookie list: nexusbank.com/cookie-list. Last updated: January 2025.
